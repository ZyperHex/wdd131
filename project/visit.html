Getting There

The Obelisk is located at the intersection of Avenida 9 de Julio and Avenida Corrientes, in the very heart of Buenos Aires. Easily accessible by subway (lines B, C, and D), buses, and on foot.

Best Times to Visit

Visit during daylight hours for the best views and photos. Nighttime offers a stunning illuminated view of the monument and its surroundings.
