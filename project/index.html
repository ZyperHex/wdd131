Fun Fact

Symbol of Buenos Aires

The Obelisk represents the heart of the city—an unmistakable reference point and gathering place for major events, celebrations, and demonstrations. Its presence reflects the city's identity, energy, and cultural pride.

Architectural Wonder

Created by renowned modernist architect Alberto Prebisch, the Obelisk showcases clean lines and monumental scale. Its bold, minimalist design broke away from traditional styles and marked a turning point in Argentina's urban aesthetic.
