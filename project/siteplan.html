Site Plan - Obelisk of Buenos Aires

Site Name: Obelisk BA: History & Significance

Obelisk BA is a website dedicated to exploring the rich history and deep cultural significance of the Obelisk of Buenos Aires, an emblematic monument of Argentina

Optional domain availability: obeliskoba.org

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide detailed information about the Obelisk's history, its architecture, the historical events it has witnessed, and its cultural significance to Argentinians. It will also include sections of curious facts, visitors information, and its presence in art and popular culture.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: What is the history behind the construction of the Obelisk, and why was it built?

Scenario 2: What significant events have taken place around the Obelisk over the years?

Scenario 3: How can I visit the Obelisk, and what nearby attractions can I find?

Color Schema

This Color Schema is based on the colors of the Argentine flag: Blue, White, and Gold. The colors light blue and white symbolize purity and peace, while the yellow sun, known as the "Sun of May," represents hope and renewal.

Primary Color: #6CACE4 (sky blue)

Secondary Color: #FFB81C (yellow/gold)

Tertiary Color: #7D4016 (brown)

Background Color: #FFFFFF (white)

Typography

Heading Font: 'Playfair Display', serif - Evokes a sense of classicism and elegance.

Body Font: 'Open Sans', sans-serif - Provides readability and clarity.

Accent Font: 'Courier Prime', monospace - For historical data and specific dates.
